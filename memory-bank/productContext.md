## Why This Project Exists

### Market Problem

Deregulated energy markets offer customers choice, but this choice creates a paradox: too many options lead to decision paralysis. Customers face:

1. **Information Overload**: Hundreds of plans with varying rate structures, terms, and fees
2. **Complexity**: Difficult to compare plans and understand true costs
3. **Fear**: Worry about making wrong decisions and overpaying
4. **Time Constraints**: Lack of time to research and compare all options
5. **Uncertainty**: Don't know if switching is beneficial given contract terms and fees

### Business Opportunity

- **Market Size**: Millions of customers in deregulated energy markets
- **Pain Point**: High customer frustration with plan selection process
- **Solution Gap**: No intelligent, personalized recommendation system exists
- **Value Proposition**: Increase conversions, improve satisfaction, reduce support burden

## Problems We Solve

### For Customers
1. **Decision Simplification**: Reduce hundreds of options to top 3 personalized recommendations
2. **Cost Transparency**: Clear calculation of projected savings
3. **Risk Awareness**: Understand when switching might not be beneficial
4. **Time Savings**: Get recommendations in seconds instead of hours of research
5. **Confidence**: Make informed decisions with plain-language explanations

### For Arbor
1. **Conversion Uplift**: 20% increase in plan sign-ups
2. **Customer Satisfaction**: 10 point NPS increase
3. **Support Efficiency**: 30% reduction in plan selection inquiries
4. **Competitive Advantage**: First-mover advantage in AI-powered energy recommendations
5. **Data Insights**: Understand customer preferences and market trends

## User Experience Goals

### Primary Experience Flow

1. **Data Input** (Simple & Fast)
   - Upload or connect 12 months of usage data
   - Enter current plan details
   - Select preferences (cost, flexibility, renewable, ratings)

2. **Recommendation Generation** (< 2 seconds)
   - System analyzes data and generates recommendations
   - Clear loading state with progress indication

3. **Results Display** (Clear & Actionable)
   - Top 3 recommendations prominently displayed
   - Projected annual savings clearly shown
   - Plain-language explanations for each recommendation
   - Risk flags and warnings if applicable
   - Easy comparison view

4. **Decision Support** (Transparent)
   - Detailed breakdown of costs
   - Contract timing considerations
   - Switching cost analysis
   - Trade-off explanations

### Design Principles

1. **Simplicity**: Clean, uncluttered interface
2. **Transparency**: Clear explanations of how recommendations are generated
3. **Trust**: Show data sources and calculation methods
4. **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 compliant for all users
5. **Mobile-First**: Responsive design for all devices
6. **Speed**: Fast, responsive interactions
